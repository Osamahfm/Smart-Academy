    What you will learn

  • You will learn how to break down complex problems into smaller, manageable parts using logical strategies.
  • You'll understand the importance of algorithmic thinking and how to approach challenges step-by-step.
  • The course will help you analyze problems efficiently by identifying patterns and choosing the right solving techniques.
  • You will practice writing pseudocode and flowcharts to plan your solutions before jumping into code.
  • You'll explore various problem-solving paradigms, such as greedy algorithms, divide and conquer, and backtracking.
  • You'll develop the ability to optimize your solutions for better time and space complexity.
  • You'll understand how to model complex systems by breaking them into smaller, manageable objects that interact with each other.
  • The course will strengthen your skills in debugging and testing, ensuring that your solutions are accurate and reliable.
  • You will solve a wide range of real-world and competitive programming problems, sharpening your critical thinking.
  • By the end of the course, you’ll gain confidence to tackle coding interviews and programming challenges with a strategic mindset.

    This course includes

  • 25 basic lectures
  • 3 assignments on every lecture
  • Recap videos
  • Quizzes on every lecture

By the end of this course you will be able to

  1. Have the critical thinking to solve problems
  2. Try to use different approaches for solving algorithms
  3. Acquire problem-solving skills
